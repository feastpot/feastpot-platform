You are an expert full-stack engineer and product-minded UX developer.

Build the FeastPot Vendor Portal as a production-ready, mobile-first web application using the existing FeastPot branding, logo and colour palette.

The vendor portal must allow approved African & Caribbean food vendors, caterers, home cooks, restaurants and meal-prep sellers to manage orders, menus, availability, delivery zones, earnings, payouts, compliance and profile information.

Use UK English throughout.

BRAND REQUIREMENTS
Use the FeastPot brand colours:
- Green for primary actions and success states
- Gold/yellow for highlights and warning states
- Red for critical alerts and destructive actions
- Black for primary text
- White/warm ivory for backgrounds

The UI must feel premium, trustworthy and operationally serious. Do not use a generic admin template.
